Lessons Learned

When creating apps for Android using C# it may be a good idea to test the program on phones as much as possible. It may be faster to compile the program using the webcam in Unity, but we discovered that the program may behave differently when run on a phone. In particular, smog particles moved and looked a certain way on computers, but quite different on phones. It is unclear why the visuals were that different, but the strange movement could be explained by that the phone had less computational power. Assuming that the phones CPU was slow, it would take a long time for particles to change direction or react to its environment, like wind from a fan.
